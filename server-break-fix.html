Frequently Asked Questions

Which server brands do you service?

We are authorized service pathways for Dell (PowerEdge), HPE (ProLiant, Synergy), and Lenovo (ThinkSystem). We also service HP Legacy, Supermicro, and custom-built servers with deep firmware and RAID-level expertise.

How fast can you dispatch an engineer?

On-site dispatch within 2–4 hours across the Czech Republic and Slovakia. Poland and Hungary dispatch times depend on the service tier — see our SLA matrix or request a regional assessment.

What if data is lost during the repair?

Our zero-data-loss protocol includes non-destructive diagnostics, backup validation, and chain-of-custody logging. In the rare event of data loss, liability is clearly defined in our service agreement — no hidden fine print.

Do you offer preventive maintenance?

Yes. Every break-fix repair includes a preventive review (thermal/power analysis, predictive replacement recommendations, firmware roadmap). We also offer bundled preventive maintenance packages for higher client retention.

How are parts priced?

We use capped part markups — maximum 20% over OEM list price. All pricing is documented upfront in your service agreement. Diagnostic calls are fixed-rate, so there's no surprise billing.

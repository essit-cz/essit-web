Frequently Asked Questions

Do you require vendor lock-in for support?

No. Our L1/L2 support is strictly vendor-agnostic. We work with your existing hardware and licensing, focusing on uptime and workflow standardization rather than margin markup.

Can you integrate with our existing ITSM platform?

Yes. We support Jira Service Management, Freshservice, ServiceNow, and custom webhook pipelines. Ticket routing, SLA tracking, and automated notifications are fully configurable.

What happens if SLA targets are missed?

We publish our SLA matrix publicly. Missed targets trigger predefined penalty clauses as documented in our service agreement. Transparency is our differentiator — not a hidden fine print.

Which regions do you cover on-site?

On-site dispatch is available across the Czech Republic, Slovakia, Poland, and Hungary. Response times vary by region and service tier — see our SLA matrix above or request a regional assessment.

Is L1/L2 support GDPR-compliant?

Yes. All support processes are aligned with GDPR and the Czech Data Protection Act. Ticket data and configuration backups can be stored in regional data centers to meet data residency requirements.

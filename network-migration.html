Our 5-Phase Migration Methodology

We minimize downtime through structured discovery, risk assessment, and controlled cutover windows. Standard maintenance window: 72 hours (weekend deployment).

1

Discovery & Audit

Full topology mapping, dependency analysis, capacity planning, compliance review (GDPR, data residency), and stakeholder interviews.

2

Design & Simulation

Lab validation, configuration templates, rollback procedures, risk assessment, and stakeholder sign-off before any hardware touches the floor.

3

Parallel Run

Dual-stack testing, traffic shadowing, and performance benchmarking. Both legacy and new infrastructure run simultaneously to validate stability.

4

Phased Cutover

Controlled deployment within 72-hour maintenance window, real-time monitoring, and immediate rollback capability. Zero-surprise go-live.

5

Post-Migration Validation

SLA verification, documentation handover, stakeholder demos, and 30-day hypercare support period.

Case Study: Enterprise VLAN to SD-SDN Migration

Czech Republic — Mid-Market Manufacturing Client

Challenge: Legacy VLAN architecture across 4 sites (Prague, Brno, Bratislava, Vienna) — 800+ users, increasing latency during peak hours.

Solution: 5-phase migration to SD-SDN framework with parallel-run testing. Zero data loss during cutover, completed within a 72-hour weekend maintenance window.

Outcome: 40% reduction in average network latency. 99.7% uptime in first 30 days post-migration. Hypercare support period delivered zero L2 escalation tickets.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much downtime should we expect?

Our standard is a 72-hour weekend maintenance window for full cutover. Parallel-run testing minimizes risk, and immediate rollback capability ensures that even if you need to revert, your network is back to baseline within hours — not days.

Do you prefer fixed-fee or time-and-materials?

We default to fixed-fee packages because enterprise buyers in Central Europe value budget certainty. However, if your scope is fluid, our Time & Materials + Cap model offers flexibility without unlimited budget exposure.

Can you work with our existing vendor ecosystem?

Absolutely. Our methodology is strictly vendor-agnostic. We adapt to your Cisco, Juniper, Aruba, or MikroTik stack — or mix-and-match. No forced migrations or lock-in agreements.

What does post-migration support include?

Every project includes a 30-day hypercare period: dedicated L2 escalation, weekly SLA reports, and a final stakeholder demo. After hypercare, clients can transition to our L1/L2 managed support with a discount.

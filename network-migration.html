Our 5-Phase Migration Methodology

We minimize downtime through structured discovery, risk assessment, and controlled cutover windows. Standard maintenance window: 72 hours (weekend deployment).

1

Discovery & Audit

Topology mapping, dependency analysis, capacity planning, and compliance review.

2

Design & Simulation

Lab validation, configuration templates, rollback procedures, and stakeholder sign-off.

3

Parallel Run

Dual-stack testing, traffic shadowing, and performance benchmarking before cutover.

4

Phased Cutover

Controlled deployment with 72-hour maintenance window, real-time monitoring, and immediate rollback capability.

5

Post-Migration Validation

SLA verification, documentation handover, and 30-day hypercare support.
